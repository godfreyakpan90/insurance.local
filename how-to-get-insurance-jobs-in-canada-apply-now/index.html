How to Get Insurance Jobs in Canada | Apply Now

Are you looking for Insurance jobs in Canada? Here are several websites that can help streamline your job search. These websites provide job listings, resources, and tools to assist you in finding the right insurance job for your career goals.

To simplify your job hunt, Insurance Business shares a list of top websites in Canada that offer excellent opportunities for finding insurance positions. These platforms grant access to top-notch job listings across all levels of the insurance profession, spanning from entry-level roles to pivotal executive positions. Furthermore, they offer a diverse set of filters to help align your qualifications and preferences with job opportunities.

Top Website to Get Insurance Jobs in Canada

#1. Indeed Canada (www.indeed.ca):

Indeed is a comprehensive job search engine that aggregates job listings from various sources, including company websites and job boards. You can search for insurance jobs in Canada and set up job alerts to receive notifications when relevant positions become available.

Indeed continues to be a highly frequented job search platform, both in Canada and worldwide, owing to its user-friendly interface and straightforward layout. With approximately 300 million unique users visiting the site each month, it’s an excellent choice for those seeking access to a vast number of job listings. In Canada, the Indeed website hosts over 5,000 insurance job postings.

Features:

  • Date posted
  • Whether the role is remote or not
  • Salary estimate
  • Job type
  • Occupation
  • Industry
  • Location
  • Company
  • Job language

#2. LinkedIn (www.linkedin.com):

LinkedIn is a valuable professional networking platform that also features a robust job search section. Many insurance companies in Canada post their job openings on LinkedIn, and you can leverage your professional network to connect with potential employers.

LinkedIn, the world’s largest professional networking platform, extends beyond being merely a job listing site due to its social media functionality. It offers a broad spectrum of digital networking possibilities for the business community. LinkedIn’s intuitive interface enables direct messaging with various job listings.

Thanks to its prominence as a business networking platform, LinkedIn boasts an extensive collection of job listings. In Canada, the website showcases over 28,000 insurance job opportunities. To streamline your search, you can make use of its diverse set of filters.

#3. InsuranceWorks (www.insuranceworks.ca):

InsuranceWorks is a specialized job board dedicated to insurance industry jobs in Canada. You can search for positions by location, job type, and company, making it a tailored resource for insurance professionals.

InsuranceWorks.ca stands as Canada’s largest specialized job search platform exclusively catering to insurance careers. With a substantial user base, the website boasts over 53,000 active registered job seekers and more than 300 registered employers. Their job board hosts approximately 600 job listings, which can be refined using a wide range of expertise-based filters. Furthermore, you have the option to organize job listings by various criteria, including:

  • Posting date
  • Location
  • Experience level
  • Employer

InsuranceWorks.ca additionally provides a section highlighting the top 10 most popularly viewed insurance job listings. This feature offers valuable insights into the roles that garner the most interest among job seekers.

#4. Workopolis (www.workopolis.com):

Workopolis is a Canadian job search platform that features job listings from various industries, including insurance. You can search for insurance-related positions and apply directly through the platform.

#5. Monster Canada (www.monster.ca):

Monster is another popular job search website in Canada that offers a wide range of job listings. You can search for insurance jobs by location and use their tools to build and upload your resume.

Monster has established itself as a trailblazer and innovator in the online employment and recruitment sector. It introduced the first resume database, which required usernames and passwords for access, setting a precedent in the industry. Monster was also a pioneer in launching a job-search agent, a concept that later became common on other job search websites. Moreover, it was the first job search engine to create a specialized category for executive-level positions.

Leveraging its extensive global reach, Monster offers a vast array of job listings, including thousands of insurance jobs throughout the country. Users can refine these listings by location, such as state, city, and insurer.

Monster also provides the option to subscribe to job alerts, ensuring you receive email notifications whenever new insurance job openings are posted. The platform features a salary tool to help you estimate compensation figures for negotiation. Additionally, Monster’s career advice section is a popular resource for job seekers.

#6. Glassdoor Canada (www.glassdoor.ca):

Glassdoor provides job listings, company reviews, and salary information. You can search for insurance job opportunities, read company reviews, and access valuable insights to aid in your job search.

#7. Insurance Institute of Canada

The Insurance Institute of Canada (IIC) is a nonprofit organization that specializes in offering standardized professional development programs for Canada’s insurance industry. While it isn’t a typical employment website, you can still conduct insurance job searches on their platform, although the job listings may not be as extensive. The Job Site section of the organization’s website features 25 job postings from 11 different insurers. If you prefer more concise listings, the IIC website might be a suitable choice for your job search. Furthermore, you can explore job opportunities in the fields of accounting and banking and financial services.

As the IIC operates a career development website, users also have access to a range of training courses, some of which enable you to earn designations in areas such as:

  • Chartered Insurance Professional (CIP)
  • Advanced CIP
  • Fellow Chartered Insurance Professional (FCIP)

#8. Jobboom

Jobboom is the top employment website in Qubec. Users, however, can still access listings from outside the province. Jobboom functions as a job board and advisory service business. Apart from job postings, the website offers contract opportunities, training, and career advice for both the jobseekers and employers. At present, Jobbooms job boardlists more than 3,500 insurance jobs across Canada.

By registering with Jobboom, you can upload and update your resume and create email alerts for job offers. You can also activate your CVs visibility to let insurance companies know that you are actively searching for opportunities.

#9. CareerBuilder Canada (www.careerbuilder.ca):

CareerBuilder is a well-established job search website that features a variety of job listings, including those in the insurance industry. You can create job alerts and upload your resume for easy application. CareerBuilder is a global HR tech platform that assists employers in talent management and recruitment. It holds a substantial international presence, including Canada, the United States, Asia, and Europe, ranking it among the largest employment websites worldwide. When conducting a brief search on their job board, you can find approximately 350 insurance job listings throughout Canada. The website offers filters that enable you to refine your search results based on various criteria.

  • The type of job
  • Date of posting
  • Salary range

CareerBuilder offers a free resume upload feature, making it convenient for insurance companies to access your resume. Additionally, it provides a section with professional advice, offering tips on various topics such as interview question responses and salary negotiation. Users can also utilize the CBSalary.com tool to compare salaries for comparable roles within the insurance industry.

#10. Insurance Bureau of Canada (IBC) Career Opportunities (www.ibc.ca):

The IBC’s website occasionally lists job opportunities within the insurance industry. It’s a useful resource to check for positions within insurance-related organizations in Canada.

#11. Job Bank

Job Bank is an employment platform operated through a collaboration between Employment and Social Development Canada and provincial as well as territorial governments. The convenience of accessing Job Bank is also available through a mobile application.

The website’s job board presently displays approximately 150 insurance job opportunities. You have the option to refine your search based on criteria such as salary, prerequisites, skills, and job role, which can be further categorized by province and territory. Notably, the website offers a valuable labor market information section, providing a concise overview of the specific position you’re interested in. This information encompasses details like:

  • The jobs description
  • Prospects across Canada
  • Job requirements
  • Median wage
  • Number of jobs available
  • Skills needed

You also have the option to subscribe to email alerts and sign up for a Plus account. This upgraded account grants you access to the Job Match tool, which helps you find positions that align with your distinct skills and abilities.

#12. Insurance-related Company Websites:

Many insurance companies in Canada post their job openings directly on their websites. If you have specific companies in mind, it’s a good idea to visit their career pages to find job listings and apply directly.

#13. ZipRecruiter

ZipRecruiter is an online job marketplace that streamlines the process for employers by allowing them to post job listings on over 100 job websites with a single submission. The platform harnesses the power of artificial intelligence to assess more than 36 million resumes in its database, enabling it to effectively match job openings with the most suitable candidates. This functionality has made ZipRecruiter a popular tool among companies and HR professionals.

For job seekers, ZipRecruiter provides access to an extensive array of job listings. You can refine your job search by applying filters to align with your experience and skills. The platform features an impressive number of nearly 30,000 job postings for insurance positions across Canada. Additionally, you can utilize ZipRecruiter’s salary tool and access job seeker reviews to aid in your job search.
